Dos and Don'ts

DON'T's

  • Ask me to draw sexual NSFW content or anything illegal or hateful

Terms of Service

Payment

  • Payment will be done through artistree request forms, and artistree/stripe invoices.
  • Prices will be in Canadian dollars.
  • All prices given on artistree and my website are estimates.
  • Your commission price will be given after I have read and accepted your request. 50% of the price will be paid before I start working on the piece. You must pay the full price before you receive the final product.
  • I do not offer refunds or cancellations.

Process & Revisions

  • I may decline your commission request.
  • Simpler pieces (ones like profiles , chibis, sketches) could be done within a week. Pieces that involve backgrounds or painted elements could take a month or more. The process will slow down significantly during the months of September to April.
  • During each step of the progress I will send WIPs and you will have chances to make specified changes to the piece. Changes count as one correspondence, so list all the changes you want to make at once.
# of Free Changes What to Change Additional Changes
Sketch 3 Pose and designs 5
Lineart 1 Small details, like adding or removing items and accessories. 8
Base Colors 2 The colors and patterns chosen. 5
Shading 1 The color of the shadows and highlights, or the shape of shadows and highlights. 10

The price for additional changes will need to be paid before you receive the final product.

Product & Rights

  • You are receiving a one time product, which is a piece of digital artwork (I do not provide physical copies).
  • You are free to use the art piece in any non-commercial setting, however you may not alter them or claim them to be yours or someone else’s work. If you choose to alter it, please disclose them when posting it on social media or similar services.
  • I reserve the rights to commissioned pieces, and will be able to use it for self promotion (e.g. portfolio, social media posts). If you do not want your commission shown, please notify me ahead of time.
