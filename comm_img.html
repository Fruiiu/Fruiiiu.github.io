Terms of Service

By commisioning me, you understand that:

Payment

  • Payment will be done through artistree request forms, and/or artistree/stripe invoices.
  • All prices given on artistree and my website are in canadian dollars and are estimates.
  • Your commission price will be given after I have accepted your request. 50% of the price will be paid before I start working on the piece. You must pay the full price (other 50% and any additional fees) before you receive the final product.
  • I do not offer refunds or cancellations.

Process & Revisions

  • I may decline your commission request.
  • Simpler pieces (ones like profiles , chibis, sketches) could be done within a week. Pieces that involve complex backgrounds or painted elements could take a month or more. The process will slow down significantly during the months of September to April.
  • During each step of the progress I will send WIPs and you will have chances to make specified changes to the piece. Changes count as one correspondence, so list all the changes you want to make at once.
# of Free Changes What to Change Additional Changes
Sketch 3 Pose and designs 5
Lineart 1 Small details, like adding or removing items and accessories. 8
Base Colors 2 The colors and patterns chosen. 5
Shading 1 The color of the shadows and highlights, or the shape of shadows and highlights. 10

The price for additional changes will need to be paid before you receive the final product.

Product & Rights

  • You are receiving a one time product, which is a piece of digital artwork (I do not provide physical copies).
  • You are free to use the art piece in any non-commercial setting. However, you may not alter the commision or claim it to be your or someone else’s work. If you choose to alter it, please disclose them when posting it on social media or similar services.
  • I reserve the rights to commissioned pieces, and will be able to use it for self promotion (e.g. portfolio, social media posts). If you do not want your commission shown, please notify me ahead of time.
  • If you are commissioning me for commercial purposes, terms must be agreed upon before work starts on the commission. Keep in mind there will be a fee for the commercial rights of my work.
